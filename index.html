What do these numbers mean?

Every field tracks a different real-world metric — there's no physical property shared by an AI benchmark, a qubit count, a fusion Q value, and a neural interface's bit rate. To plot them on one 0–100 axis, each metric is normalized against an anchor that represents a widely-cited "done" threshold for that field. The number is a comparison of momentum, not a claim that the fields are equivalent.
